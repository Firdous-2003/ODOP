Discover the Art of Tradition with ODOP

The ODOP initiative in Jammu and Kashmir is all about promoting and showcasing the unique products of this region including traditional handicraft, textile, carpets, shawls among others. This Government lead initiative aims to support local artisians and entreprenurs, boost the local economy and create job opportunities.

Experience Traditional Handicraft with ODOP

At ODOP, we are dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of India through traditional handicrafts. We provide an exclusive platform for artisans to showcase their unique creations, from handmade shawls to exquisite carpets, and elegant showpieces, all while ensuring authenticity through blockchain authorization and biometric authentication.
